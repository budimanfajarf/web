## About The Project

Nomaden Worker is a website that helps you find your perfect workspace. It is a curated list of public spaces suitable for working, such as cafes, libraries, co-working spaces, and more. You can select your needs and preferences, such as seating type, facilities, ambience, and payment options, and the website will show you where you can find them.
